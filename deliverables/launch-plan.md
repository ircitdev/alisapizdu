# PLAN VIRUSNOGO ZAPUSKA: "Alisa Pokazhi Pizdu"

**Dokument:** Detalniy plan virusnogo zapuska
**Data sozdaniya:** 2026-03-21
**Target launch:** ~2026-04-04 (chetverg)
**Byudzhet:** 0 rub (tolko organika)

---

## 1. TIZER-STRATEGIYA (za 5 dney do zapuska)

### Kontseptsiya tizera
Sozdat intrugu vokrug domena, ne raskryvaya sam sayt. Domen — eto uzhe gotoviy mem. Zadacha — zastavit lyudey zapomnit URL i zhdat otkrytiya.

### Den -5 (voskresene, 30 marta)
**Format:** Skrinshot pokupki domena
- Post v Twitter/X: "Kupil domen. Ne mogu poverit chto on byl svoboden." + skrinshot registratsii domena (zamazat domennoe imya, no chtoby ugadyvalsy nabor bukv)
- Tseli: lyubopytstvo, reposty "chto tam za domen?"
- Kolichestvo: 1 post, otvetov na kommentarii ne davat

### Den -4 (ponedelnik, 31 marta)
**Format:** "Sluchayno" raskryt domen
- Post v Twitter/X: "Ok, mne pishut 'pokazhi domen'. Ironichno." + skrinshot adresoy stroki s domenom
- Telegram: post v 2-3 IT-chatah ot svoego imeni "Rebyata, ya kupil etot domen i ne znayu chto s nim delat. Idei?"
- Tseli: pervaya volna repostov, lyudi nachinayut delitsya domenom kak memom

### Den -3 (vtornik, 1 aprelya)
**VAZHNO: 1 Aprelya — Den Duraka. Ispolzovat ostorozhno.**
- Ne zapuskat sayt v etot den — reshit chto eto shutka i zabyt
- Mozhno ispolzovat: "Net, eto ne prikol na 1 aprelya. Eto realniy proekt."
- Post: "Menya sprashivayut — eto shutka na 1 aprelya? Net. Ya realno delayu sayt gde neuroset otvechaet na ETOT vopros. Zapusk cherez 3 dnya."

### Den -2 (sreda, 2 aprelya)
**Format:** Perviy skrinshot chata
- Pokazat 1 otvet "Alisy" — samiy smeshnoy iz zagotovlennyh
- Post: "Testiruyu. Ona uzhe otvechaet. Zapusk poslezavtra."
- Skrinshot dolzhen byt v temnoy teme, s vidimym domenom v shapke
- Tseli: lyudi nachinayut sohranyat i delitsya skrinshtotom — eto uzhe gotoviy mem

### Den -1 (chetverg, 3 aprelya)
**Format:** Obratniy otschet
- Twitter/X: "Zavtra v 12:00 MSK zapusk alisapizdu. Serezno."
- Telegram: "Zavtra v polden. Podpishites na kanal [ssylka] chtoby ne propustit."
- Esli est Telegram-kanal proekta — opublikovat pravila i ob'yasnenie formata

### Klyuchevoy printsip tizerki
- NIKOGDA ne ob'yasnyat polnostyu chto eto — pust lyudi peresprashivayut
- Kazhdiy post dolzhen soderzhat TOLKO odin noviy fakt
- Otvetov na voprosy davat minimum — "uvidite na zapuske"

## 2. PLOSHCHADKI ZAPUSKA (s prioritetami)

### PRIORITET 1 (den zapuska, pervye 2 chasa)

#### Twitter/X russkoyazychniy
- **Pochemu #1:** Samaya bystraya ploshchadka dlya viralnosti v RU-segmente
- **Format:** Korotkiy post + skrinshot chata + ssylka
- **Taktika:** Zapostit ot svoego akkaunta, poprosit 5-10 druzey (esli est) sdelat retweet v pervye 5 minut
- **Heshtegi:** ne ispolzovat heshtegi — oni snishayut organiku v X algoritme
- **Kolichestvo postov:** 1 osnovnoy + 1 reply s kontekstom

#### Telegram
- **Pochemu #1 bis:** Samaya massovaya ploshchadka RU-interneta
- **Kuda postit (besplatno):**
  - IT-chaty: @proglib_chat, @frontend_ru, @devops_ru — kak lichnoe soobshchenie "rebyata, ya sdelal vot eto, posmotrite"
  - Mem-chaty: lubiye otkrytye chaty krupnyh mem-kanalov
  - Konfoy: razrabotchikov, dizaynerov, studentov
  - Svoy kanal proekta (esli sozdan): zapustit tam pervym
- **Kuda predlozhit post (besplatno):**
  - @ithumor (600k+) — predlozhit cherez bota skrinshot otveta
  - @memes (krupnye mem-kanaly) — predlozhit cherez bota
  - @kod (programmirovanie) — kak "petproekt"
  - Kanaly pro AI/neurosetii — "smotrite chto ya sdelal s AI"

#### Pikabu
- **Pochemu vazhno:** 10M+ mesyachnyh posetiteley, UGC-formata
- **Format posta:** Razdel "IT-yumor" ili "Bayanometer"
- **Zagolovok:** memnyy, korotkiy (sm. razdel 3)
- **Telo posta:** 2-3 skrinshotov samyh smeshnyh otvetov + ssylka
- **Vremya:** 12:00-14:00 MSK (obedenniy pik)
- **VAZHNO:** Ne davat ssylku v pervom predlozhenii — Pikabu ne lyubit reklamu. Snachala razvlech, ssylka v kontse

### PRIORITET 2 (den zapuska, chasy 3-6)

#### VKontakte
- **Format:** Post v soobshchestvo (sozdat esli net) + reposts v publiki
- **Kuda predlozhit novost:**
  - Lepra (esli est dostup)
  - IT-yumor VK
  - MDK / Orhidey (esli podhodyat po formatu)
  - Studencheskie publiki
- **Osobennost VK:** Algoritm VK v 2026 otdaet prioritet engagement-u, ne vremeni. Kontent dolzhen provocirogat kommentarii

#### vc.ru
- **Format:** Statiya-keys "Kak ya zapustil sayt alisapizdu i chto iz etogo vyshlo"
- **Ton:** Poluironichnyy, no s realnymi metrikami
- **Kogda:** Na 2-3 den posle zapuska, kogda uzhe est tsifry
- **Rubrika:** "Tribuna" (bez moderatsii)
- **VAZHNO:** Na vc.ru tsenitsya storytelling. Ne prosto "vot sayt", a "pochemu ya eto sdelal, skolko bylo zaprosov za perviy den, reakcii"

### PRIORITET 3 (dni 2-5)

#### Habr
- **Format:** Tehnicheskiy post "Kak ya sdelal real-time chat s neurosetyu za [N] dney"
- **Fokus:** Arhitektura, WebSocket, AI-prompts, nagruzka
- **Yumor:** Vtoroplannyy, glavnoe — tehnicheskie detali
- **VAZHNO:** Habr karmaet (soobshchestvo golosuet). Chistaya reklama = minus-karma. Tehnicheskiy kontexst = plus-karma s viralnym effektom
- **Vremya publikatsii:** Vtornik-chetverg, 10:00-12:00 MSK

#### Reddit (r/Pikabu, r/ru)
- **Realnost:** Reddit v Rossii dostupna tolko cherez VPN s 2025
- **Auditoriya:** Mala (5-20 postov/den), no kachestvennaya
- **Format:** Skrinshot + ssylka, memnyy zagolovok
- **Prioritet:** Nizkiy. Delat tolko esli est akkaunt s karmoy

#### TikTok / YouTube Shorts / VK Clips
- **Format:** Screen-recording zapusk saytz: nazhimaesh knopku -> zhdiosh -> chitaesh otvet -> smeyoshsya
- **Zvuk:** Reaktsiya (smeh, udivlenie) ili trendoviy zvuk
- **Tekst na video:** "Posmotrite chto otvetila Alisa" (bez upominaniya domena — pust sprashivayut v kommentariyah)
- **Kolichestvo:** 3-5 korotkih video s raznymi otvetami
- **Kogda:** Vtoraya polovina dnya zapuska i kazhdiy den pervoy nedeli
- **VAZHNO:** V TikTok ne davat ssylku v video — davat v bio. Kommentariy "gde eto?" = organichnyy boost

### PRIORITET 4 (dlya amplifikatsii)

#### DTF.ru
- Razdel "Flud" ili "Kreativ" — skrinshoty otvetov
- Auditoriya peresekaetsya s geymerami i IT

#### Yandex Dzen (dopolnitelno)
- Ironichno: ispolzovat platformu Yandeksa dlya prodvizheniya saytz pro "Alisu"
- Format: "Alisa otvechaet na samyy populyarnyy vopros rossiyskogo interneta"

## 5. VIRUSNAYA MEHANIKA VNUTRI PRODUKTA

### 5.1. Skrinshoty kotorye sami sebya prodayut

**Princip:** Kazhdiy skrinshot chata dolzhen soderzhat vsyu informatsiyu dlya togo chtoby chelovek zahotal zayti na sayt.

**Chto dolzhno byt vidno na lyubom skrinshote:**
- Domen v shapke saytz (krupno, kontrastno)
- Schetchik "Sprosili N raz" — sotsialnoe dokazatelstvo
- Schetchik "Onlayn N" — FOMO
- Temnaya tema — horoshjo smotritsa v postah i stories
- Sam otvet — smeshnoy, chitaemyy

**Tekhnicheskaya realizatsiya:**
- Domen v shapke — fixed, vsegda vidim
- Schetchiki — animated, obnovlyayutsya real-time
- Shrift otveta — dostatochno krupnyy dlya szhatiyz pri skrinshote
- Kontrast — belyy tekst na temnom fone (kak v Telegram night mode)

### 5.2. Knopka sheriniga (ne "podeli", a "skopirovay")

**Ne nado:** Standartnye knopki "share on Twitter/VK/etc" — ikh nikto ne zhmet

**Nado:**
- Knopka "Skopirovat otvet" ryadom s kazhdym otvetom Alisy
- Pri nazhatii kopiruetsya tekst formata:
  ```
  Ya: Alisa pokazhi pizdu
  Alisa: [tekst otveta]

  alisapizdu.ru | Sprosili uzhe N raz
  ```
- Eto legko vstavit v chat, v post, v kommentariy
- Taknzhe knopka "Skachat kak kartinku" — generiruet skrinshot-kartinku s brendingom

### 5.3. Schetchiki kak FOMO-mashina

**Schetchik "Sprosili N raz":**
- Dolzhen byt krupnym i animirovannym
- Kazhdoe novoe nazhatie — schetchik krasivok provorachivaetsya
- Pri krugliyh tsifrakh (1000, 5000, 10000) — spetsialniy effekt
- "Kto-to tolko chto sprosil" — tooltip/toast v uglu ekrana

**Schetchik "Onlayn N":**
- Zeleniy indikator, pulsiruyushchiy
- Pokazyvaet realnoe kolichestvo WebSocket-soedineniy
- Esli onlayn > 100 — dobavit tekst "GORYACHEE"
- Esli onlayn > 500 — "V TRENDE"
- Esli onlayn > 1000 — "VZRYV"

### 5.4. Lenta chata kak zrelishche

- Novye soobshcheniya poyavlyayutsya v real-time dlya VSEH posetiteley
- Chelovek prikhodit na sayt i vidit KAK drugie lyudi zhalyut knopku
- Effekt stadiona — ty chast tolpy
- Samye smeshnye otvety mozhno "laiknyt" (serdechko) — eto vliyaet na "Top otvetov"

### 5.5. Milestone-soobshcheniya

Kogda schetchik dostigaet krugloy tsifry, v chat poyavlyaetsya spetsialnoe soobshchenie:
- 100 zaprosov: "Sto popytok, nol uspehov. Alisa nepreklonna."
- 1,000: "Tysyachu raz sprosili. Ona ni razu ne soglasila. Legenda."
- 10,000: "10 tysyach chelovek sprosili Alisu pokazat pizdu. Internet v poryadke."
- 100,000: "Sto tysyach. Eto oficialno samaya chasthaya prosba k neurosethi v istorii."

## 6. KONTENT-PLAN POSLE ZAPUSKA

### Nedelya 1: Momentum

**Ezhednevno:**
- 1 post v Telegram-kanal s "otvetom dnya" (samiy smeshnoy otvet za sutki)
- 1 post v Twitter s obnovlennym schetchikom
- 1 TikTok/Shorts s novymi otvetami

**Cherez den:**
- "Top-5 otvetov Alisy za segodnya" — gotoviy format dlya Pikabu/VK

### Nedelya 2: Uglublenie

**Serialnyy kontent:**
- "Alisa otvetila na ETO" — seriya postov s samymi neozhidannymi otvetami
- "Filosofskaya Alisa" — kogda neuroset dait glubokiy/umnyy otkaz
- "Derszkaya Alisa" — kogda neuroset trollit polzovatelya
- "Pechalenaya Alisa" — kogda neuroset otvetila grustenym otkazom

**UGC-strategiya:**
- Dobavit na sayt razdel "Zal slavy" — luchshie otvety po golossovaniyu
- Prizyvat (myagko) delitsya skrinshtotami: "Esli vam ponravilsya otvet — delites, nam priyanno"
- Otslezhivat upominaniya v sotssetyah, repostit luchshie

### Nedelya 3-4: Evolyutsiya

**Novye mehaniki (besplatnyy launch):**
- "Rezhim bota" — Alisa otvechaet goloshom (TTS)
- "Sravnenie" — pokazat kak raznye neurosetii otkazyvayut na odin vopros
- "Statistika" — publichnaya stranitsa so vsemi metrikami (skolko raz sprosili, iz kakih gorodov, v kakoe vremya pik)

**PR-hody:**
- Statiya "1 million zaprosov: anatomiya virusnogo saytz" (esli dostigli)
- Intervyu/podcast u IT-blogerov (predlozhit besplatno, eto interesniy keys)

### Mesyac 2+: Monetizatsiya (Faza 2)

**Zapusk platnykh soobshcheniy:**
- Knopka "Napisat svoy vopros — 1000 rub"
- Announcement v Telegram-kanale i na sayte
- A/B test: 500 rub vs 1000 rub vs 2000 rub
- Kontentnyy formatir: "Kto-to zaplatil 1000 rub chtoby sprosit Alisu [vopros]. Vot chto ona otvetila"

### Kakie tsifry = uspeh

**Minimum viable success (MVP):**
- D1: 1,000+ unikalnyh posetiteley
- D1: 3,000+ nazhatiy knopki
- D7: 10,000+ summarnyrh posetiteley za nedelyu
- D7: 50+ organicheskikh upominaniy v sotssetyah (bez vashego uchastiya)
- Bounce rate < 60% (lyudi ostayutsya smotret chat)

**Horoshiy rezultat:**
- D1: 5,000-10,000 posetiteley
- D7: 50,000+ summyrno
- 10+ organicheskikh postov na Pikabu/Twitter s 100+ laykami
- Kto-to sdelal mem/video pro sayt bez vashey prosyby

**Virusniy vzryv:**
- D1: 50,000+ posetiteley
- D7: 200,000+
- Popali v top Pikabu / trending Twitter RU
- SMI napisali pro sayt
- Yandeks otreagiroval (lyuboy reaktsiyey)

### Kogda pannikovat

**Krasnye flagi v pervyy den:**
- Menee 200 posetiteley za pervye 6 chasov -> strategiya poseva ne srabotala, nuzhna eksrennaya korrektsiya
- Bounce rate > 85% -> sayt ne tsepliyet, problemy s UX
- Sredni otvet AI > 10 sekund -> neuroset tormozit, lyudi uhhodyat
- 0 repostov/upominaniy za 4 chasa -> kontexnt ne virusnyy, nuzhno menyat zagolovki

**Deystviya pri panike:**
1. Proverit chto ssylka rabotaet (bazovoe, no vazhnoe)
2. Smenit zagolovok/tekst posta i zapostit zanovo na drugie ploshchadki
3. Lishno napisat 20-30 znakomym s prosboi posmotret i podelitsya
4. Sdelat bolee provokatsionniy skrinshot i zapostit v drugie chaty
5. Esli nichego ne rabotaet za 24 chasa — ne pannikovat, a sobrat feedback: "pochemu ne sharite?"

## 8. ANTI-CRISIS PLAN

### 8.1. Yandeks prislal pretenziyu po brendu "Alisa"

**Veroyatnost:** Srednyaya (30-50%), rastet s populyarnostyu saytz

**Prevenitsiya (do zapuska):**
- Na sayte dolzhen byt disclaimer: "Sayt ne imeet otnosheniya k Yandex i golosovomu pomoshchniku Alisa"
- Ne ispolzovat logotip Yandeksa ili ofitsialnyye tsveta Alisy
- Stilizatsiya pod Alisu — da, no ne kopirovanie
- Podgotovit alternativniy brand zaranee (sm. nizhe)

**Plan rebrenda (esli pridet pretenziya):**
1. **Immediative response (24 chasa):** Otvetit "Spasibo za obrashchenie, izuchaem"
2. **Rebranding (48 chasov):**
   - Smenit imya s "Alisa" na alternativu:
     - "Anfisa" (zvuchit pohoze, no ne brand Yandeksa)
     - "Luisa" (smeshnoe zvuchanie)
     - "NeAlisa" (meta-yumor, podcherkivaet chto eto NE Alisa)
     - "AI-SA" (abbreviatura, sohranyaet zvuchanie)
   - Obnovit domein? Net — domen ostavit, on uzhe viral. Prosto ubrat upominaniya "Alisa" s saytz
3. **PR-hod:** Prevratitr pretenziyu v kontent: "Yandeks napisal nam pretenziyu. Teper Alisa — eto Anfisa. Ona po-prezhnemu ne pokazyvaet."
4. **Yuridicheski:** Parodiya zaschischena kak fair use v bolshinstve yurisdiksiy, no luchshe ne suditsya s Yandeksom — proshche rebranding

**Klyucheviy prinsip:** Pretenziya ot Yandeksa = BESPLATNIY PR. Eto luchshee chto mozhet sluchitsya dlya virusnosti. "Yandeks zametil alisapizdu" — eto zagolovok kotoriy sam sebya shire.

### 8.2. Negativ v media / sotssetyah

**Stsenariy A: "Eto vulgarno/nepristoyno"**
- Reakciya: Ne oborontysya. Otvetit s yumorom: "My sozdali sayt gde neuroset uchit vezhlivomu otkazu. Eto edukativnyy proekt."
- Ne udalyat negativnye kommentarii — oni generiruyut engagement
- Ne vstupat v spory — odin otvet i ignorirovat

**Stsenariy B: "Eto seksizm/obektifikatsiya"**
- Reakciya: "Sayt demonstriruet kak AI gramotnno otkazyvaet na neumentnye zaprosy. Kazhdiy otvet — eto urok graniets."
- Seryozno otnestis k kritike, ne trollit
- Esli kritichesskaya massa — dobavit na sayt "O proekte" s ob'yasneniem yumora

**Stsenariy C: "Eto prostoo gluposti"**
- Luchshaya reaktsiya: "Da. Imenno." Samoironiya — luchshaya zashchita

### 8.3. Habr-effekt (vnezapnaya nagruzka)

**Podgotovka (do zapuska):**
- Nagruzochnoe testirovanie: minimum 500 odnovremennyh WebSocket-soedineniy
- CDN dlya statiki (Cloudflare besplatnyy)
- Rate limiting na API neurosetii (ne bolee N zaprosov v minutu)
- Graceful degradation: esli AI peregruzhen, pokazat sluchainiy otvet iz kesha
- Queue sistema: esli ochered > N, pokazat "Alisa dumaet... Pered vami N chelovek v ocheredi"

**Kommunikatsiya pri padenii:**
- V Twitter/Telegram: "Sayt upall pod nagruzkoy. Vy slishkom mnogo prosili Alisu pokazat pizdu. Chistim, skoro budem"
- Na samom sayte (esli chastichno rabotaet): "Nas slishkom mnogo. Alisa v shoke. Vozvrashaytes cherez 5 minut"
- NIKOGDA ne pishat "tehnicheskiye problemy" — pisat s yumorom v dukhe proekta
- Padenie = PR: "Sayt ne vyderzhall [N] chelovek odnovremenno" — eto zagolovok

**Deystviya pri peregruzke:**
1. Vklyuchit queue (ochered) vmesto otkaza
2. Uvelichit cache otvetov AI (pochti-dubli = ok)
3. Vremenno otklyuchit WebSocket i perevesti v polling (rezhe obnovlyat chat)
4. Esli API neurosetii dostig limita — rotatsiya provayderov (OpenAI -> Anthropic -> lokalnaya model)
5. V krainnem sluchae — vklyuchit "read-only" rezhim: pokazyvat tolko predydushchie otvety

### 8.4. Zhaloby na kontent (hosting/registrator)

**Prevenitsiya:**
- Hosting: ispolzovat VPS (ne shared hosting gde mogut otklyuchit po zhalobe)
- Registrator: ispolzovat zarubezhnyy registrator domena (Namecheap, Cloudflare) — menshhe shansov na otklyuchenie po lokalnym zhalobam
- Kontent na sayte: sam sayt NE sodershit nepristoiniy kontent — tolko vopros i yumornye otkazy

**Esli domen zablokiruyut:**
- Zapasnyye domeny (kupit zaranee): alisanet.ru, sprosialisu.ru
- Redirect za 1 chas
- Noviy domen = noviy infopovodov: "Nam zablokirovali domen — my pereehali"

## ITOGO: KLYUCHEVYE PRINTSIPY

1. **Domen = marketing.** Lyudyam ne nuzhno ob'yasnyat chto eto — URL govorit sam za sebya
2. **Kazhdiy otvet = gotoviy mem.** Skrinshot dolzhen byt samodostotochnym
3. **Ne prosit sharit — davat povod sharit.** Smeshniy kontexnt sham sebe dorogy
4. **Falling = PR.** Lyuboy "krizis" (padenie, pretenziya, kritika) = besplatniy infopovodov
5. **Kontentnyy konveyer.** Kazhdiy den est noviy otvet Alisy = noviy post = novyi prichina zayti
6. **Zero-budget, maximum hustle.** Lichnye soobshcheniya, chaty, predlozheniya v kanaly — eto rabota, no besplatanaya
7. **Metriki > oschushcheniya.** Smotret na tsifry, ne na "kazhetsa idet horosho"
